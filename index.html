Conclusion

Final Verdict

Based on the two-sample Z-test for proportions (Z = 7.3701, p = 0.0001), there is overwhelming statistical evidence to reject the null hypothesis. The advertisement (AD group) produced a conversion rate of 2.55%, compared to 1.79% for the public service announcement (PSA group).

This translates to a 42.5% relative improvement in conversions. We recommend fully deploying the advertisement campaign and leveraging peak engagement windows (Mon–Tue, 11:00–15:00) to maximize its effectiveness.
